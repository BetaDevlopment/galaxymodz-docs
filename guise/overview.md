# Guise — Overview

**Guise** is a GalaxyModz plugin that lets players and admins change their visual appearance — disguising as other players, mobs, or custom skin presets — without affecting their actual identity or data.

---

## What It Does

Guise is purely cosmetic. The player's real username, UUID, and all server-side data remain unchanged. Only what other players (and optionally the wearer themselves) see is affected.

**Key Features:**

- Disguise as any player or entity type
- Custom skin support via preset names
- Configurable self-view (see your own disguise or not)
- Configurable disguise removal on death or world change
- Permission-based access control
- Lightweight with minimal performance impact

---

## Use Cases

- Roleplay and adventure map servers
- Staff undercover moderation
- Custom cosmetic systems for minigames

---

## Requirements

- Java 17+
- Paper / Spigot 1.20+
- [ProtocolLib](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/protocollib.1997/) (required dependency)
