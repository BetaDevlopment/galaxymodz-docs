# BambEat — FAQ

**Q: Does BambEat work with custom food plugins?**
A: It may conflict with plugins that override item use events. Test on a staging server first.

---

**Q: Can I disable the eating sound?**
A: Currently the vanilla eating sound plays. A toggle for this is planned in a future update.

---

**Q: Can pandas also benefit from the config values?**
A: BambEat targets player consumption only. Vanilla panda behaviour is unaffected.

---

**Q: How do I remove all effects?**
A: Set `effects` to an empty list in `config.yml`:
```yaml
effects: []
```

---

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact **GalaxyModz** or **Frostbyte** via your support channel.
