# BambEat — Overview

**BambEat** is a GalaxyModz plugin that makes bamboo edible for players. Hunger restoration, saturation, potion effects, and cooldowns are all fully configurable.

---

## What It Does

By default, bamboo cannot be eaten in vanilla Minecraft. BambEat adds that behaviour — players simply hold bamboo and right-click to consume it, just like any food item. Everything about the eating mechanic is controlled through `config.yml`.

**Key Features:**

- Makes bamboo consumable by players
- Custom crunchy bamboo eating sounds
- Block-break particles while eating
- Configurable hunger and saturation values
- Optional potion effects on consumption
- Eat cooldown support
- Permission-gated eating
- **Geyser Extension available** — full Bedrock eating animation support (v1.1.0+)
